# Data Story 2
This data story explore how global increase in pasturelands drives the effects of land use change especially in its regards to climate. As more land becomes devoted to rearing livestock, carbon sequestering ecosystems are lost, and intense amounts of resources become needed to raise the animals. This story looks at India as a case study for a country will relatively little pasturland, low agricultural emissions, and high agricultural efficiency.
